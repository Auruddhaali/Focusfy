## ğŸ® How to Use

### Getting Started
1. **Launch FocusFy** from Desktop or Start Menu
2. **Add your first task** by typing and pressing Enter
3. **Set a timer** using the preset buttons
4. **Start focusing** by clicking the Start button
5. **Toggle theme** if you prefer light mode

### Floating Timer
1. Click **"Show Floating Timer"** in the sidebar
2. The timer appears in the top-right corner
3. **Drag it** anywhere on your screen
4. It **stays on top** of other windows
5. Click **"Hide Floating Timer"** to close it

### Theme Switching
1. Find the **theme toggle** in the sidebar
2. Click to **switch** between Dark and Light modes
3. Your preference is **automatically saved**

### Task Management
1. **Add**: Type task and press Enter or click Add
2. **Complete**: Click the checkbox to mark done
3. **Delete**: Hover over task and click the trash icon
4. **Review**: Check your completed tasks count in stats
